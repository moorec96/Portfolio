Akia's Mind

Akia's Mind is a science fiction short story that I wrote in my BCORE class during my first quarter at UW Bothell. Out of approximately 50 students, my story, as well as 8 other students stories, were chosen to be published for the following years textbook. This is the story of man against robot in a not too far off future where the machines have realized that the only logical way to bring peace to Earth, is to destroy the emotionaly unstable humans. The story was published in The Monolith Volume IV.
