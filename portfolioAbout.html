About Me

Since I was a child, I have always been one who is constantly letting my imagination explore. For a huge part of my years growing up, I wanted to be an author. I wrote countless fantasy and sci-fi short stories during my elementry and middle school years. The idea of creating a whole new reality where everything it contained was in my control was exhilerating to me. Being able to work on a project where I can allow my imagination to roam free is one of the key aspects that I look for when it comes to choosing a career to pursue. Since my younger years, I have allowed my imagination to branch out into new areas. I have explored music, writing, game development, drawing, and programming most recently. I began to learn how to code during my senior year of highschool and since then have continued to pursue this field. Programming is fun to me because what you can produce with it is endless. Whether you make a fitness tracker app or develop a website for Nasa, programming always has a place for the developers imagination to explore. This website's purpose, other than to show my credentials, is to give you a glimpse into my mind.
